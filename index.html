Intro

Hi, my name is Janine

and I made this website because I feel like everyone in my field has one nowadays.

I'm a master's student at the Hertie School of Governance in Berlin taking up Data Science for Public Policy. I specialize in quantitative methods for policy and economic research, focusing on antitrust policy and regulation.
